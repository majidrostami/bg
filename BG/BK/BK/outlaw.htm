Character Control:

Mouse
  • Move mouse within playing area to move character.
  • Left click mouse to shoot.
  • Right click mouse to reload.
Keyboard
  • Use left, up, right, and down arrows to move character.
  • Use space bar to shoot.
  • Use R to reload.


Rules:

Each outlaw kill earns you one point. Use barrels, cactuses, tombstones, and tumbleweeds as protection. Shooting these items destroys them but does not earn you or cause you to lose any points. You have three lives and no time limit. Objective is to earn the largest number of points before game is over. Doing so will get you name on the scoreboard.
